# Your Beat Store — Getting Started

Welcome! Your beat store is built and ready. There are just a few small things you need to do on your end to get everything live and start making money.

---

## What you need to do

### 1. Buy a domain name (~$12/year)

This is your website address (e.g. `yourname.com`).

1. Go to [Namecheap.com](https://namecheap.com)
2. Search for the domain name you want
3. Add it to cart and purchase it
4. **Send me the domain name** — I will connect it to your site for you

> Tip: `.com` is always the best choice. Keep it short and easy to remember.

---

### 2. Create a PayPal Business account (free)

This is how you receive money when someone buys a beat.

1. Go to [paypal.com](https://paypal.com) and sign up
2. Choose **Business account**
3. Complete the verification steps (takes 5–10 minutes)
4. **Send me your PayPal email address** — I will connect it to your store so payments go directly to you

---

### 3. Set up Railway (your hosting — ~$5/month)

Railway is where your website runs 24/7.

1. Go to [railway.app](https://railway.app)
2. Sign up with GitHub (create a free GitHub account first if you don't have one)
3. Add a payment method under **Billing**
4. **Let me know when this is done** — I will deploy your site to your Railway account

---

### 4. Connect your domain

Once I've deployed your site, I'll send you two DNS values to paste into Namecheap. It takes 2 minutes:

1. Log in to [Namecheap.com](https://namecheap.com)
2. Go to **Domain List** → click **Manage** next to your domain
3. Click **Advanced DNS**
4. I'll send you the exact records to add — just copy and paste them in
5. Your site will be live within a few minutes

---

## After your site is live — uploading beats

You have a private admin panel at `yoursite.com/admin/referrals` (I'll send you the exact link).

To upload a beat:

1. Log in to your site
2. Go to your admin panel
3. Fill in the beat details — title, BPM, key, genre, prices per license tier
4. Upload your cover art and audio preview (MP3)
5. Paste in your Google Drive download links for each license tier (Basic, Premium, Unlimited, Exclusive)
6. Click **Add Beat** — it goes live on your store immediately
