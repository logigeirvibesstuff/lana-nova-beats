# Your Beat Store — Setup Guide

Your website is ready. There are only 2 things you need to do before it goes live.

---

## Step 1 — Buy a domain name (~$12/year)

This is your website address, e.g. `yourname.com`

1. Go to **[namecheap.com](https://namecheap.com)**
2. Search for the name you want
3. Purchase it (`.com` recommended)
4. Send me your domain name

I will connect it to your site.

> Example: `johnnybeats.com`, `trapkingbeats.com`

---

## Step 2 — Create a PayPal Business account (free)

This is how you get paid when someone buys a beat.

1. Go to **[paypal.com](https://paypal.com)**
2. Click **Sign Up** → choose **Business Account**
3. Fill in your details and verify your account
4. Send me your PayPal email address

All payments go directly to you.

---

## That's it.

Once I receive your domain name and PayPal email, I'll get everything set up and send you:

- Your live website link
- Your private admin link to upload beats
- Your login details

**From there, uploading a beat takes 2 minutes.**
